Help Center

How do I add a transaction?
Go to the “Add/Edit” page, fill out the form with the transaction details, and click Save to add it.
How do I edit or delete a transaction?
Use the Edit and Delete buttons next to each record.
What does the chart show?
The chart displays your spending trends over the last 7 days, broken down by each day of the week.
How do I change currencies?
Go to the Settings page, choose your preferred currency, and enter the manual exchange rates to convert amounts.
